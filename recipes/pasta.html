Homemade Pasta

Homemade pasta dough is a fun and delicious way to step up your pasta game! With just 2 ingredients, spirit, and a lot of kneading you will be able to enjoy this dish that people have been making for hundreds of years.

ETA

Total: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 2 Egg

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 to 2 cups of flour onto a counter in a pile and make a hole in the middle of the pile so it looks like a volcano.
  2. Crack 2 eggs and put them in the center of the "volcano."
  3. Using a fork, start whisking the eggs together while slowly incorporating some flour each whisk.
  4. Once the eggs turn too solid to whisk, begin kneading the mixture with your hands. (Make sure they are washed, of course!)
  5. After 5-15 minutes, a smooth dough will form. Once this happens, wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  6. After it is done sitting, cut the dough in half and roll each half until thin.
  7. Cut the dough into 1/4 inch strands and toss the strands together with flour.
  8. From here you are finished! You can either freeze the dough for another day or cook it now. When cooking the dough remember, homemade pasta cooks much faster than store-bought pasta and the pasta water you cook the pasta in is full of flavor and starch that can be used for another dish.
